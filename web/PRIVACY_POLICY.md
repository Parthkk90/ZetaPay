# Privacy Policy for ZetaPay Browser Extension

**Last Updated:** November 5, 2025  
**Effective Date:** November 5, 2025

## Introduction

ZetaPay ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the ZetaPay browser extension.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Wallet Addresses**: We temporarily store your connected wallet address locally in your browser to facilitate transactions.
- **Transaction Data**: Order details, payment amounts, and merchant information are processed locally and transmitted directly to the blockchain.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Browser Information**: Basic browser type and version for compatibility purposes.
- **Extension Usage**: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the extension (can be disabled in settings).

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- **Private Keys**: We never access, store, or transmit your private keys or seed phrases.
- **Personal Information**: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
- **Browsing History**: We do not track or store your browsing history beyond detecting checkout pages.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Facilitate cryptocurrency payments on e-commerce platforms
- Display transaction status and history within the extension
- Improve extension functionality and user experience
- Provide customer support when requested

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: All sensitive data (wallet addresses, transaction history) is stored locally in your browser using Chrome/Firefox storage APIs.
- **Blockchain**: Transaction data is recorded on the ZetaChain blockchain, which is public and immutable.
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not store your data on our servers.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only:
- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize us to do so.
- **For Legal Compliance**: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.
- **With Service Providers**: Third-party blockchain networks (ZetaChain) and wallet providers (MetaMask, etc.) as necessary to provide the service.

## Third-Party Services

ZetaPay integrates with:
- **ZetaChain**: Blockchain network for processing transactions
- **Wallet Providers**: MetaMask, WalletConnect, and other Web3 wallets
- **Payment Processors**: (Future) Razorpay, PayU for fiat settlements

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- All transactions require explicit user approval via wallet signature
- No storage of private keys or sensitive credentials
- Encrypted communication with blockchain networks
- Regular security audits and updates

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored locally by the extension
- **Delete**: Clear all extension data from browser settings
- **Opt-Out**: Disable analytics and usage tracking in extension settings
- **Disconnect**: Remove wallet connections at any time

## Children's Privacy

ZetaPay is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## International Users

ZetaPay is designed for global use. By using the extension, you consent to the transfer of data to blockchain networks which may be located in different jurisdictions.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via:
- Extension update notifications
- Updates to this document with revised "Last Updated" date
